Stockton College

Reintroducing a regional asset

Published: Feb 12, 2010

A public liberal arts college located in Southern New Jersey in the National Pinelands Reserve, serving around 8,000 students and know as New Jersey’s green college, seeks to reintroduce itself, its growing campus, and the community it has become.

Reintroduce the college by establishing a sense of place and community

Stockton College found itself defined more by its early 70’s days as a fledgling upstart, than by its current reality as a leading regional public liberal arts college. In an attempt to correct this, while also drawing in more students unfamiliar with the college and its beautiful Pinelands setting, the site was built around creating a sense of place that helped update the image.
